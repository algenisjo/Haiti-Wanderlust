Minimal Water Filter

A simple and effective way to filter water with natural items.

Any container to be filled with (bottom to top): 1. Charcoal, no bigger than the size of a finger nail but not to thin either where it will clog (half rice). 2. Tiny pebbles slightly bigger than sand. 3. Coarse sand.

Find water from a source to fill up container periodically. Too much water at once will clog the system. Repeat this process twice to better filter it and get rid of contaminants. Then boil the water to purify it.
